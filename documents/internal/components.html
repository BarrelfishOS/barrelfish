Overview of ECLiPSe components and assignment of maintainers

Author: Joachim Schimpf
Date: 2002-02-07

Purpose

This document gives an overview of all components of the ECLiPSe system and assigns two or more maintainers to each component. The intentions are
  • every change by one maintainer is reviewed (possibly after check-in) by the/an other maintainer
  • every change by a non-maintainer should be reviewed by a maintainer before check-in
  • at every team meeting, we go through the recent CVS commit log and make maintainers aware of what needs to be reviewed
Abbreviations used:
